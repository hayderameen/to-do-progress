This is a To-Do list app. To learn more about it, click on Learn More button below.

How to use To-Do App

Step 1: Enter your username in given input field and click on "Add User" button.

Step 2: Enter any task in the given task input field and click on "Add Task" button.

Step 3: After completing any task, click on any of tasks in "Non-Completed list" and it will go into completed list automatically!
